Dentene - Branding

The brief was to brand a plain white Toothbrush. The process first started with creating a Brand Map which documented the Brand philosophy, the strategy, vision and the reach. The logo then designed is such that it reflects the Brand values and the personality of the brand. Visiting cards and posters too were designed for the brand. The final packaging was designed keeping the Brand values in mind and maintains a Minimal and clean aesthetic to it.
