Smart Kitchen App - UI/UX design

A home is said to be Smart when equipped with lighting, heating, and electronic devices that can be controlled remotely by smartphone or computer. A Smart Kitchen performs a similar task wherein the devices like electronics, lighting and heating can be controlled remotely. The brief was to design an Application that could be used to control the present Smart devices for the Kitchen available in the market. It can be used to control devices which performs functions like cooking, cleaning, washing and even pantry management.
